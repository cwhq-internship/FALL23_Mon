About us

CodeWizardsHQ gives students the necessary skills to build platforms like this one. Over the course of several years of study in live, online coding classes, students acquired the necessary skills to develop and deploy a full-stack web application that helps users solve real-world problems.

Students learn skills necessary to become successful back end developers, such as Python, Flask, and databases. They also spend several courses learning and applying front end concepts such as design frameworks, interactive webpages, and more. As a culmination of all of their CodeWizardsHQ coursework, students apply for and are selected to participate in a months-long group project.
